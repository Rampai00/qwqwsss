Our Culture: Botho in Action

We embrace Botho — the Sesotho philosophy of humanity, respect, and community. Together we grow.

Botho (Humanity)

We treat each other with dignity, compassion, and respect.

Khotso (Peace)

We foster harmony and collaboration in all interactions.

Kopano (Unity)

Together we achieve more through collective effort.

Tsoelopele (Progress)

Continuous growth and learning for all Basotho.
